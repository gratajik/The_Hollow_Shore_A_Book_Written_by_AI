## Places

### Blackwood Island, Maine
- ~2 miles long × 1 mile wide
- Rocky coast, dense pine forest interior, single hill with mansion at peak
- Ferry service from Bar Harbor (45-minute ride)
- Boathouse, private dock, helicopter pad, formal gardens (overgrown), carriage house, groundskeeper's cottage
- **Smuggler's caves beneath island** — entrance hidden; "Classroom," "Preparation Chamber," "Memory Dissolution Tank," "Integration Suite"
- "The hollow shore" — specific location within cave system; ritual site

### Blackwood House
- Victorian mansion, built 1890s
- Three stories, turrets, wraparound porch
- Grand entrance hall, library, Sarah's preserved bedroom, basement storage, attic
- Hidden passages typical of Victorian
- Blue wallpaper in Maya's guest room — triggers first memory fragment
